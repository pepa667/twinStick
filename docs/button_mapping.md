
# Universal Button Mapping

TwinStick uses an internal neutral mapping that is converted to different HID standards.

## Internal Mapping

| Button | ID |
|------|----|
UP | 0 |
DOWN | 1 |
LEFT | 2 |
RIGHT | 3 |
A | 4 |
B | 5 |
X | 6 |
Y | 7 |
START | 8 |
SELECT | 9 |
L1 | 10 |
R1 | 11 |
L2 | 12 |
R2 | 13 |
HOME | 14 |

## XInput Mapping

A → A  
B → B  
X → X  
Y → Y  
L1 → LB  
R1 → RB  

## Nintendo Switch Mapping

A → B  
B → A  
X → Y  
Y → X  

## DInput Mapping

A → Button1  
B → Button2  
X → Button3  
Y → Button4
